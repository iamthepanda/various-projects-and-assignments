Art

I lied. There isn't any more. But read on if you want to know more about me.

My parents wanted me to become an architect

I received drawing lessons when I was young. I didn't much care for it then, so I never put any effort in it. My parents posted every drawing and every painting of mine on the living room wall for all of our guests to see. I tore them all down and threw them away.

In high school I joined a program called AOIT (Academy of Information Technology). One of our modules was to learn how to use Photoshop to do Graphics Design. I reused my brother's old projects from when he was in the class.

Years later, I now find that I quite enjoy drawing and creating things that look cool. But now I kind of suck at drawing. I misplaced or deleted all my old personal projects so now I can't even showcase it on this website.

It is not too late to start over though. The Assassin's Creed logo I put on my site is a recreation of another project that I had lost. Drawing is not so easy to remaster, but with time and practice, I am certain that I can once again become proficient at that as well.

Thanks for reading!
