Jone

My family name, Jone, is a Transliteration of the name 鍾. Other adaptations of the name include: Zhong, Chung, Cheong, or Jong

I am not entirely sure why my dad chose such a unique spelling of the name, especially because all of my extended family uses 'Zhong'. I suspect that it is because it is better for business since it sounds more American.

It confuses many people when I tell them my last name. In grade school, some people would ask me if I was adopted. Others would ask me if I am mixed race. It complicates things a bit further since I have curly hair, which is a rare trait for East Asians (I get it from my mother!).

In the end I have come to like how unique this name is. It is a huge part of my identity and has had a major contribution in shaping who I am
